I founded Kurios (YC S21) — a digital and AI cohort-based training platform for enterprises. Scaled it from zero to ~$1M ARR with 20+ Tier-1 clients across 8 countries, raised $2.7M, and built a 36-person team.

I think in systems. I care deeply about what actually gets adopted. I've run GTM, product discovery, roadmap, design, and hiring — not because I had to, but because I wanted to understand every layer.
